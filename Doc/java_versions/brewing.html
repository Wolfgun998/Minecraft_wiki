Brewing

Brewing is the process of creating potions, splash potions, and lingering potions by adding various ingredients to water bottles in a brewing stand.
- To make a stronger potion just brew it with glowstone dust. This well make the potion stronger but it will last less.
- To make a portion stay longher brew it with redstone.
