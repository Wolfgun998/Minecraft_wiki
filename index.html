Minecraft

Minecraft is a 3D sandbox adventure game developed by Mojang Studios where players can interact with a fully customizable three-dimensional world made of blocks and entities.
Its diverse gameplay options allow players to choose the way they play, creating countless possibilities.

There are four editions of Minecraft even created:

  • Java Edition (Windows, macOS, Linux)
  • Bedrock Edition (Windows, mobile and consoles)
  • New Nintendo 3DS Edition (Nintendo 3DS e 2DS)
  • Legacy Edition (Nintendo Switch, Xbox 360, Xbox One, PlayStation 3 e 4, PlayStation Vita, Wii U)
